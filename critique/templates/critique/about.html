About

During my time at DreamWorks, I always wanted a better way to keep up with my notes from dailies. We had our own notebooks of course, and we were given various online tools over the years. But none of our tools ever had a way to easily associate an image, let alone label the areas of interest. This is a quick Django/Jquery proof-of-concept for such a tool.

I completed this project in two weeks using Django 1.8 with Python 2.7, MySQL and Jquery. Limitations of the public app include:

  • The app was built for desktop browsers only, and is not mobile-friendly
  • The first critique in the list cannot be deleted
  • No more than three critiques can exist at one time
  • Image upload and replacement is disabled
